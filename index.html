Simple Omelette Recipe

An omelette is a dish made from beaten eggs, cooked in a pan and often filled with ingredients like cheese, vegetables, or meats. Itâ€™s a quick, versatile, and delicious meal

Prepartion Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minute
  • Cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
